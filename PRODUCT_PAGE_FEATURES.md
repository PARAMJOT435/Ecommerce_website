## Color Coding

### Status Indicators
- 🟢 **Green** - In stock, good status
- 🔵 **Blue** - Information, delivery details
- 🟡 **Yellow** - Warning, low stock
- 🔴 **Red** - Out of stock, urgent

### Icon Colors (Benefits)
- 🔵 Blue - Shield (warranty)
- ❤️ Red - Heart (authenticity)
- 🟢 Green - Check (returns)
- 🟣 Purple - Gift (support)

## Responsive Behavior

### Mobile (< 768px)
- Full width layout
- Highlights: 2 columns
- Sidebar: Below main content
- Related products: 1 column
- Specs: Scrollable
- All sections stack vertically

### Tablet (768px - 1024px)
- Two-column with 320px sidebar
- Highlights: 2-3 columns
- Related products: 2 columns
- Most content side-by-side

### Desktop (> 1024px)
- Two-column with 320px sidebar
- Highlights: 4 columns
- Related products: 4 columns
- Maximum width container
- Optimal reading distance

## Usage Tips

### For Customers
1. **Check Highlights** - Quick overview of key benefits at the top
2. **Check Delivery Info** - See delivery timeline and stock status on the right
3. **Read FAQ** - Expand FAQ items to answer questions without contacting support
4. **View Related Products** - See similar products at the bottom
5. **Leave Reviews** - Share your experience after purchase

### For Store Owners
1. **Customize Highlights** - Match your selling points
2. **Update Warranty Info** - Keep delivery and warranty details current
3. **Add Product-Specific FAQs** - Can be made dynamic per product
4. **Monitor Related Products** - Same category products shown automatically
5. **Gather Reviews** - Build social proof
